About

This is a simple expense tracker application that allows you to add, edit, and delete expenses.

You can add expenses by selecting a category, entering a description, entering an amount, and entering a date.

You can edit expenses by clicking the edit button on the expense row.

You can delete expenses by clicking the delete button on the expense row.

You can filter expenses as well as see them in graphs and charts, and export them to a PDF or CSV file.
